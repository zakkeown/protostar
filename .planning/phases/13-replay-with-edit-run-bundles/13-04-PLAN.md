Wire the read-set sidecar at the **review iteration** boundary, at the **pile** writer (covering ALL four current pile call sites in main.ts), and reconcile bundle layout so 13-ADR-001 documents both review-related sidecar locations as distinct read-set surfaces. This plan enumerates and pins all four current pile call sites.

Output: review-iter and pile sidecars committed alongside existing artifacts at every call site; reuses `writePathsReadSidecar` from plan 13-03; ADR-001 amended.
